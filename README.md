## Overview

If you're not careful and you **noclip** out of reality in the wrong areas, you'll end up in the Backrooms... A place where things don't look and feel the same compared to the Overworld.

The Backrooms may warp reality in such a weird way, that stores, houses, parks, and so on, may defy the laws of life. These warped places often contain a **null zone**... A null zone is, as said, a zone, where anything at anytime can go through and get to the Backrooms...
